I’m an actor and writer. Mhm. Some of the questions I often come back to, directly or indirectly: what is the maximum amount of self-deception, in which we can delight and indulge, before becoming destructive? 1 how can sloppy communication amount to something that’s like sort of actually precise? 2 can we hope our way out of violence? 3 where do tragedy and comedy meet? 4 where do they stray? how much should be left to the audience? 5 where is the love switch? why are we crying? why do they think that? why not? 6

Originally from California, I'm currently based in Glasgow, where I'm working towards an M.F.A. in Acting at the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland. Before that, I graduated Magna cum Laude from Columbia University, with a B.A. in Drama and Creative Writing.
